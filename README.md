HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI
📌 Project Introduction

This Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive insights into an organisation's workforce by analysing employee distribution, job satisfaction, promotions, retrenchment risk, and workforce demographics.

The dashboard helps HR teams monitor employee performance indicators and identify employees eligible for promotion or retrenchment, enabling data-driven workforce management decisions.

🔎 Key Business Questions

What is the overall workforce distribution by gender and job role?

How satisfied are employees with their current job roles?

Which employees are due for promotion?

Which employees may be at risk of retrenchment?

How does employee tenure and distance from office impact workforce trends?

Which departments have the highest promotion or retrenchment indicators?

🛠 Tools Used

Power BI Desktop

Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)

Data Modeling

Interactive Dashboard Design

📈 Key Insights

The organization has 1,470 total employees, with 60% male and 40% female representation.

1,353 employees are currently active, representing 92% of the workforce.

72 employees are identified as due for promotion based on defined criteria.

117 employees are approaching retrenchment or retirement indicators.

The majority of employees report high job satisfaction levels.

A significant portion of employees live very close to the office, which may positively impact retention and attendance.
